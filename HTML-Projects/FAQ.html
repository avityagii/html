Student Portal - Frequently Asked Questions

Login Issues

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click on "Forgot Password" at the login page and follow the instructions.

Course Registration

Q: When does course registration open?
A: Every semester, registration opens mid-July and December.

Grades
